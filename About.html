It is a group of crazy Travellers who are fond of wandering in the great Himalayan mountain range.Our group contains 5 people: Ameya, Omkar, Shreyas, Abhishek & Suyash, all being engineering students in Mumbai and Pune. All of us have travelled together in the various regions of Himalaya present in India and Nepal and captured memories. This site came with an idea to replicate our awesome memories in the form of a website.

Our clear vision is to give a thrilling experiance to those who can't go to these places physically and to give a kick to those who are planning to visit any of these places. This try has made us to capture more and more stunning photographs and we will be trying to give our best to have an upgraded quality collection.
